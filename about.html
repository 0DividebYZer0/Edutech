Divyansh Sharma

Founder

He has been involved with community projects from a very young age. He has conducted various workshops and seminars on a wide range of topic in various NGOs across Delhi. He has a great zeal to work for the society and believes that education and technology are the 2 tools, which, if integrated together, can completely transform a person's life and open up a wide array off opportunities. He started this foundation with the sole purpose of making the resources required to excel in the 21st century easily available irrespective of the financial status of a person.
